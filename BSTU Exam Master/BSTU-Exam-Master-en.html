About a project

Our goal

We are a group of first-year students of the Faculty of information technologies of the Belarussian State Technological University. We were extremely woried about the up-coming first exam session and set a goal for ourselves: to develop an application that could help another freshmen to prepare themselves better. At the moment the application is in an alpha-test and from all of the represented disciplines only "The basics of program engineering" is available. The rest of the options are at the development stage and will soon be ready for beeng released.

Realisation

This project has been developedby our team from 08.12.2023 to 25.12.2023. And from 25.12.2023 to 29.12.2023 was in a stage of testing and bugg fixing.And since 29.12.2023 is ready for usage.
